Matthew Murphys Top Animes

My Top 3 Animes

  1. Reincarnated as a slime

    • This anime is about a Japanese office worker who is reincarnated as a slime in a fantasy world.
      It is really good and I highly recommend it.

  2. Kimitsu No Yaiba

    • This is a great action-adventure series with amazing animation and storytelling.
      if you are looking for a show that has beautful art work and amazing fight scenes, this is the one for you!

  3. Farming in another world

    • Another reincarnated anime where the protagonist builds a farm in a fantasy world.
      This is more of a slow paced show that has a few funny moments, but really brings the charm of the genre to life!
